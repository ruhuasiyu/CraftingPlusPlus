Crafting++

🎉Crafting++ is a Minecraft datapack. It contains hundreds of items and a skyisland mode. It makes the experience more useful. It also contains many plants, food and decorations.

Download, copy it into the datapacks / resourcepacks folders and enable them. This datapack supports Chinese (simplified and traditional) and English.

You need put a hopper in offhand to store your experience to Experience Bottles. Use Crafting Machine to craft if neccessary (green background).

Create a flat world such as minecraft:air;minecraft:mountains; to enable the skyisland mode.

Use /loot give @s loot [Namespace ID] to obtain items, e.g. /loot give @s loot cpp:all_in_one_machine.

Generation

Flower World

This dimension contains two biomes: flower plain and coral ocean. There are lush caves, dripstone caves, amethyst geodes, aquifers, noise caves, deepslates, and buried treasure, shipwreck and structures of this datapack.

Dark animals and hostile mobs cannot be generated here. The time is fixed at noon.

In overworld or flower world, form eight tall flowers as an octagon, and put a white wool in the center, the portal will actived. The portal is one-way.

Enchanting Room

Generated on the surface of the flower world, which consists of 12 empty bookshelves, an enchanting table and an illusion.

There are at most 3 stacks of items in every empty bookshelf, which may be papers, books, maps, compasses, random structure maps (buried treasure, ocean ruin, or shipwreck) and ancient scrolls.

Totem Pillar

Generated in the flower world, which has height at most 240 and consists of stone bricks, lava, ores, Moon Stones and chests. The chests may contain Soul of Dirt, nova of fire, limb of Ridge, Enchanted Golden Apple, Enchanted Iron, Enchanted Gold, meat floss bread, Toffee Apple, coal, bones, rotten flesh, gunpowder and string.

Fruit Tree

Generated on the surface of the flower world. The sharp is similar to birch trees with oak logs. There may be some fruits under the tree.

Ore Tree

Generated on the surface of the flower world. The sharp is similar to birch trees with spruce logs. The leaves will become ores slowly.

Wool Tree

Generated on the surface of the flower world. The sharp is similar to birch trees with birch logs. The leaves will drop wool saplings, wools, fishes or sheeps.

Sakura Tree

Generated on the surface of the flower world. The sharp is similar to birch trees with jungle logs. The leaves will drop sakura saplings or cherries.

Field

Generated on the surface of the flower world, which consists of wheats, potatoes, carrots and beetroots, or tomatoes, strawberries, blueberries and pineapples.

Flowers Bushes

Generated on the surface of the flower world, which consists of several flowers or grasses.

/scoreboard players set $disableGrassGenerate cppConfig 0 or 1 to enable/disable the generation of hybridable flowers.

Entities

Enhance Mobs

Zombies and skeletons may be armed with better armors and weapons or potion arrows.

Zombies can break torches, lanterns, end rods and sea pickles instantly.

Creepers have a lower fuse time and the powered creepers can be spawned.

scoreboard players set $doZombieImproving cppConfig 0 or 1 to disable/enable zombie enchancement.

scoreboard players set $doSkeletonImproving cppConfig 0 or 1 to disable/enable skeleton enchancement.

scoreboard players set $doWitherSkeletonImproving cppConfig 0 or 1 to disable/enable wither skeleton enchancement.

scoreboard players set $doCreeperImproving cppConfig 0 or 1 to disable/enable creeper enchancement.

Shard of the Darkness cpp:shard_of_the_darkness

Some animals become dark animals, there are black particles under their heads. They will close to and attact players. They will drop the shard of the darkness.

scoreboard players set $doDarkAnimalSpawning cppConfig 0 or 1 to disable/enable the generation.
